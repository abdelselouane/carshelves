What Makes LINZESS Different?

LINZESS is the first and only treatment in a class of drugs for IBS-C and CIC called guanylate cyclase C, or GC-C, agonists. It works differently than other available medications.

LINZESS is a capsule you take once every day to manage your symptoms. And only LINZESS is FDA approved to treat IBS-C in both men and women.
